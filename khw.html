The family that holidays together stays happier together!

In the global village that is our world today, we desire to explore new places and people, their culture and heritage while strengthening our bond with our loved ones. Holidaying is the perfect way to do them all. Our tryst with undiscovered rendezvous seems to be our basis for travelling and exploring and this need of ours is far from being satiated.

To help make holidays beautiful and stress-free KKavion Holiday World endeavours to be a personalised holiday planner and help the patron design their own vacation keeping their needs and requirements in mind. With our skilled personnel and meticulous hard work in preparing for them a customised holiday itinerary, we wish to build a relationship that lasts a lifetime. Destination and Resort information, tailored travel itinerary, amazing offers & rates, booking assistance and travel aid, would all be covered and ready to be accessed by our patrons in the comfort and convenience of their homes!

By emphasizing significant locations and features that a visitor must explore, we would obtain the twin benefits of boosting business by accentuating its best parts and also furnishing the viewer with verified and suitable information that would aid them in planning their dream vacations, thus helping us achieve our goal of presenting them with a ‘happy holiday’.

Project Milestone

Analysis is that stage of the process where after the requirement study, the project plan is made and approved while suitable designs are decided upon for it to follow through to the Development phase. After our meticulous efforts at segregating the plenteous resort and destination options and planning out how to make them easily accessible to our clientele while offering a value proposition, KKavion Holiday World is now in this stage where it is being thoroughly examined by the implementing agencies to ascertain the functionality and decide on the design of the platform..

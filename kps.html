Home is where the heart is and a happier home makes a happier you!

Buying or selling a property is always a hassle and limited knowledge in the field can often have us making unwise decisions. That coupled with multiple differing opinions with no insight on the matter proves to be the ultimate recipe for a disaster!

To make this process easier KKavion Properties envisions building a platform where both ends of the spectrum converge to provide ease of access to its patrons. Creating a virtual space that stores verified data and provides the viewer with suitable options to build their dream home is our end goal.

What facilitates this is the personal guidance that is offered to each patron. By providing hassle-free assistance in suggesting properties from a galore of options and in-depth knowledge about them & their neighbourhoods as per the required budget, we attempt to dissuade our customers from just buying a house and instead aid them in finding their dream homes by adding a human touch to help them through it all.

Project Milestone

Planning is that stage of the process where based on the findings of the Research phase project planning is done with objectives and the course of action being decided. KKavion Properties is currently in this stage where we are determined to create a proposal that would benefit both the buyers and the sellers and provide easier access to each other.

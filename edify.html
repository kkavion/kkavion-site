Edify

  • Home
  • EDIFY

Map out your career to be where you want to be.

Choosing a career option that employs and hones our skills is easily one of the biggest, not to mention the most crucial decision of our lives. With the emerging concept of a virtual workspace, the opportunities are plenteous but that makes the task of deciding on one even more difficult. Ensuring growth in a career is a whole new dimension altogether!

Edify seeks to create a platform where our patrons would find personal guidance from professionals with insight into their desired fields. We endeavour to engineer the growth of our clientele in their respective careers by planning the future course to have them follow a path that would aid them in attaining their aspirations.

Inferring their field of interest after assessing them as early as in high school, we wish to provide them with exposure to the nature of work in different professions to help them choose a path. Once into a particular profession, we desire to plan out their entire career trajectory and steer our patrons the right way to achieve their goals. To set a target and persistently pursue it throughout their work-life by planning ahead based on professional advice, we envision shaping human resources that are not only skilled but also happy with their careers.

Project Milestone

Initiation is that stage of the process where the project is assessed to check for its feasibility, set its objectives & boundaries and prospective partners are decided. Edify is currently in this stage where we are looking for suitable strategic partners to commence the project.

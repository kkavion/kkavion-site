Corporate Sales Manager

x

Location: Kolkata (In-person work)

Job Type: Full-time

Position Overview

The ideal candidate would be a dynamic and results-driven person with a strong ability to build and maintain relationships with corporate clients, with experience in corporate sales or business development. This role requires strategic thinking, excellent negotiation skills, and a target-oriented approach to drive revenue growth. If you have a passion for sales, client relationship management, and achieving business objectives, we encourage you to apply.

Qualification & Experience

  1. Education: Graduate in any discipline.
  2. Experience: Minimum of 2 to 3 years in corporate sales, business development, or a similar role.

Required Skills

  1. Strong verbal and written communication skills.
  2. Proven ability to manage corporate relationships and close high-value deals.
  3. Excellent negotiation and persuasion skills.
  4. Goal-oriented mindset with a focus on achieving and surpassing targets.
  5. Analytical skills to assess market trends and business opportunities.
  6. Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  7. Proficiency in CRM software and sales tracking tools.

Job Responsibilities

  1. Lead, guide, train, and develop a team to achieve targets through proper distribution & monitoring of job responsibilities and successful teamwork.
  2. Develop and implement sales strategies to acquire and retain corporate clients.
  3. Identify new business opportunities and generate leads through networking and market research.
  4. Address client concerns and build and maintain strong relationships with them.
  5. Conduct sales presentations, negotiations, and contract discussions with potential clients.
  6. Collaborate with internal teams to tailor solutions that meet client needs.
  7. Monitor market trends, competitor activities, and industry developments to stay ahead in the market.
  8. Achieve and exceed sales targets while ensuring high levels of client satisfaction.
  9. Maintain accurate records and reports to track engagement and performance metrics.
  10. Responsible for compiling and generating periodic reports and MIS.

Accountabilities

  1. Adhering to SOP and strengthening relationships with existing and potential corporate clients.
  2. Driving client engagement and retention strategies effectively.
  3. Ensuring timely follow-ups and smooth execution of corporate deals.
  4. Meeting and exceeding sales targets through client acquisition.
  5. Identifying new opportunities to expand the corporate sales portfolio.

Organizational Alignment

  1. Reports to: Director-Operation
  2. Works closely with: Sales & Marketing Team, Business Development Team
  3. Leads: Corporate Client Engagement Initiatives

Benefits

  1. Opportunities for talent development through exposure to corporate sales strategies.
  2. Continuous skill enhancement and service excellence through practical expertise.
  3. Mentorship that fosters career growth through continuous commitment and contributions.
  4. Performance-based incentives.
  5. Annual increments based on skill development, performance, and results delivered.

Compensation

Competitive remuneration commensurate with experience and skills.

Senior Content Writer

x

Location: Kolkata (In-person work)

Job Type: Full-time

Position Overview

The ideal candidate should have a strong command of language, excellent research skills, and the ability to craft compelling and high-quality content across multiple formats. This role involves strategizing, writing, and refining content that aligns with the company’s goals while ensuring accuracy, originality, and engagement. If you have a passion for writing, a keen eye for detail, and the ability to create impactful content, we invite you to apply.

Qualification & Experience

  1. Education: Graduate in English, Journalism, Communications, or a related field.
  2. Experience: Minimum of 2-3 years in content writing, copywriting, or a similar role.

Required Skills

  1. Exceptional writing, editing, and proofreading skills.
  2. Strong research abilities to ensure accurate and insightful content.
  3. Ability to write across various formats, including blogs, website copy, social media, and long-form content.
  4. Familiarity with SEO best practices and content optimization techniques.
  5. Creativity, adaptability, and strong attention to detail.
  6. Experience with content management systems (CMS) and writing tools.
  7. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a team setting.

Job Responsibilities

  1. Strategically plan and manage content creation by defining what, how, and when to communicate with customers, members, and clients, ensuring a consistent and impactful content flow.
  2. Develop high-quality, engaging, and well-researched content for websites, blogs, marketing materials, and other platforms.
  3. Conduct in-depth research on industry trends, competitors, and target audiences to create data-driven and impactful content.
  4. Ensure all content is original, plagiarism-free, and not AI-generated to maintain authenticity and copyright eligibility.
  5. Edit and proofread content rigorously to ensure accuracy, consistency, and adherence to brand guidelines.
  6. Optimize content for SEO and audience engagement, enhancing visibility and reach.
  7. Collaborate with marketing and design teams to develop content strategies that strengthen brand positioning.
  8. Manage multiple content projects efficiently, ensuring deadlines are met without compromising quality.
  9. Maintain a consistent brand voice and messaging, aligning content with business objectives and audience needs.
  10. Mentor and provide constructive feedback to junior writers and interns, fostering skill development and content excellence.

Accountabilities

  1. Delivering high-quality and engaging content that is plagiarism-free and not AI-generated.
  2. Conducting thorough research and creating data-driven, relevant content.
  3. Ensuring brand consistency in tone, language, and messaging.
  4. Meeting project deadlines and maintaining efficiency.
  5. Contributing to content strategy and brand positioning.

Organizational Alignment

  1. Reports to: Director-Operation / Content Manager
  2. Works closely with: Marketing Team, Design Team, SEO Specialists and Junior Writers
  3. Supports: Content Strategy and Brand Development

Benefits

  1. Opportunities for professional growth and skill enhancement through content development.
  2. Continuous learning and exposure to diverse writing styles and industries.
  3. Mentorship that fosters career advancement through commitment and contribution.
  4. Annual increments based on skill development, performance, and content impact.
  5. Performance-based incentives.

Compensation

Competitive remuneration commensurate with experience and skills.

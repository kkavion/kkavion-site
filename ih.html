Instaholiday

  • INSTAHOLIDAY

Insta-holidays are the perfect antidote to stressful urban living.

To get away from our busy lives every once in a while is just the best feeling ever! Quiet solitary retreats, for a day or two, away from the hustle and bustle of the cities are just the places to be to break the monotony and do something different and refreshing. The undiscovered nooks and corners of the outskirts offer the best possible experiences to revive the soul to get back to daily life, renewed and refreshed!

Instaholiday is a platform that provides the best-suited holiday options in the vicinity for the patron to take off on a much-needed break. With select destinations, resorts and holiday interests that will keep them rediscovering their passions, we wish to have them engaged and coming back for more.

It endeavours to showcase each destination and property by highlighting its features and focusing on the various leisure, wellness and gourmet options for the city dweller opting for a getaway. The assortment of offers along with a unique perspective on sale is what would ensure repeated patronage and leave a member feeling gratified.

Project Milestone

Implementation is that stage of the process where the project finally takes shape and becomes visible to others after it has been encoded and designed. Evaluation takes place to determine whether the result corresponds to the design and all the requirements have been met. Having browsed through numerous destination and resort options and selected suitable ones that could provide a desired break while offering an opportunity for rediscovering your interests, Instaholiday is currently in this stage where along with the uploading of data, it is being continuously probed and tested before its launch.

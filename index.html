Strategic Intent

To tap the immense latent opportunities that exist by creating synergy between products and services curated from diverse verticals and generating demand to meet and exceed the expectation of patrons.

We firmly believe that the endeavour has the potential to grow and serve great number of people by enabling them to make an informed choice to derive value proposition to enrich their lives.

With its intrinsic desire, Kkavion Enterprise aims to serve the people and businesses with a humane approach by aligning the thought process of all its stakeholders in providing customer-centric services.

KKavion Enterprise’s goal is to create an eco-system comprising individuals and businesses to leverage the economies of scale and offer the very best to its patrons in diverse domains such as hospitality, housing, lifestyle, education and talent management.

Vision

Kkavion Enterprise would specialise in curating different products and services in diverse verticals to generate demand for the same.

We would like to reach out to serve 10,000 people in the city of Kolkata with its customer-centric services in diverse verticals.

We are dedicated to build processes based on the various functions of sales & marketing to fulfil the needs and aspirations of individuals and businesses.

Mission

Provide its patron with customer-centric services in diverse verticals such as Hospitality, Housing, Lifestyle, Education and Talent Management.

Its customer-centric approach with humane touch for whom customers are just not a “customer code” instead a ‘real’ being whose needs and requirements are to be taken care of, and in the process develop a long lasting relationship that would be cherished and valued by both i.e., the organisation and by its patron.

Brand Promise

To empower people by providing solutions through customer-centric services in diverse verticals.

To enable individuals and businesses to realise value proposition that would enrich their lives.

Provide intuitive services through continuous improvement to deliver value proposition and build an endearing relationship.

Credo

We would be accountable for our responsibility, self-critical of our actions to earn the trust by providing customer-centric services in pursuit of excellence to Enrich lives
